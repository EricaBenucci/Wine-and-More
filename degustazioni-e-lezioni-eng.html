Tastings

Tastings include an introductory narration about the historical, cultural and production background of the wine or product, with a strong focus on the sensory experience: color, aromas, taste, tactile sensations and emotions.

Chianti: the flavors of Tuscany

A virtual journey through the Chianti hills to discover the character, grapes, and balance of this iconic wine. A guided tasting to appreciate its style and uniqueness.

Brunello di Montalcino: the noble red

This prestigious Tuscan wine is a symbol of elegance and refinement. Its intense aromas and exclusivity have made it a worldwide icon.

Tuscan wines

An overview of Tuscany’s finest reds and whites, with a focus on native grape varieties and terroirs. A tasting to reveal their character and magic.

Wines from the Italian mountains

High-altitude wines from unique places. We explore how elevation and mountain climates influence aromas, acidity and elegance.

Wines from the Italian islands

A journey through marine aromas, volcanic soils, and one-of-a-kind microclimates. A tasting of sunny, salty wines deeply connected to their island origins.

Iconic Italian wines: excellence from North to South

A journey along the Italian peninsula to discover symbolic wines. History, stories, and a guided tasting of some of Italy’s most celebrated labels.

Grappa, an excellence of Italian distillation

From grape skins to glass: a journey into the history and production of grappa, the only distillate that’s purely Italian. A guided sensory experience to explore its many nuances and styles.

Extra virgin olive oil

How to recognize a quality EVOO: cultivars, production techniques, tasting methods and food pairings. A surprising and educational experience.

Classes

Theoretical knowledge to understand wine and other products in depth. Lessons are followed by a guided tasting. If requested, a final test with a certificate of participation can be included.

Wine and tasting technique

An introduction to professional tasting: how to taste, which senses are involved, how to read sensations and recognize quality and faults.

Chianti uncovered

Chianti has fascinating origins and a deep-rooted tradition. It tells the story of hills, soils, microclimates, and native grapes. A guided experience to understand labels and types.

Food and wine pairing

Discover rules and methods to enhance food through wine, choose the right pairing, and create a harmonious menu.
