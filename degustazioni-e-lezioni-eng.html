Tastings and Lessons

Tastings

Each tasting begins with an introduction to the historical, cultural and production background of the product, followed by a strong focus on the sensory experience: color, aromas, taste, sensations and emotions.

1. Chianti: Flavours of Tuscany

A virtual journey through the Chianti hills to explore the personality, grapes and balance of this renowned wine. A guided tasting to appreciate its style and character.

2. Brunello di Montalcino: the iconic red

Celebrated for its refined character, this wine has become a true global icon.

3. Tuscan premium wines

An overview of Tuscany’s great red and white wines, their regions and native grape varieties. A tasting to capture their distinctive qualities and magic.

4. Wines from the Italian mountains

Wines from high altitudes and unique landscapes. Discover how mountain climates influence aroma, freshness and finesse.

5. Wines from the Italian islands

Explore distinctive island wines shaped by warm climates and sea breezes.

6. Premium Italian Wines: excellence from North to South

A tour of Italy through some of its most iconic wines. Discover the stories, curiosities and taste of Italy’s wine heritage.

7. Grappa: the Italian spirit

A journey into the history and production of Italy’s most iconic distillate. A guided tasting to explore its nuances and regional identities.

8. Extra Virgin Olive Oil

How to recognise quality extra virgin olive oil: varieties, production methods, tasting techniques and food pairings. A surprising and instructive tasting.
