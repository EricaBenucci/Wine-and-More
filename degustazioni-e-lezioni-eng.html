Tastings and Lessons

Tastings

Each tasting begins with an introduction to the historical, cultural and production background of the product, followed by a strong focus on the sensory experience: color, aromas, taste, sensations and emotions.

1. Chianti: Flavours of Tuscany

A virtual journey through the Chianti hills to explore the personality, grapes and balance of this renowned wine. A guided tasting to appreciate its style and character.

2. Brunello di Montalcino: the iconic red

This award-winning Tuscan red is a symbol of elegance and nobility. Its rich aromas and refined profile have earned it international acclaim.

3. Tuscan wines

A panorama of Tuscany's great reds and whites, focusing on native grape varieties and terroirs. A tasting to discover their uniqueness and beauty.

4. Wines from the Italian Alps

Wines from high altitudes and unique landscapes. Discover how mountain climates influence aroma, freshness and finesse.

5. Wines from the Italian Islands

A journey through volcanic soils, marine breezes and unique microclimates. A tasting of sun-kissed, mineral-rich wines deeply rooted in their island origins.

6. Famous Italian Wines: excellence from North to South

A tour of Italy through some of its most iconic wines. Discover the stories, peculiarities and taste of Italy’s wine heritage.

7. Grappa: the Italian spirit

From grape skins to the glass: a journey into the history and production of Italy’s most iconic distillate. A guided tasting to explore its nuances and regional identities.

8. Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Learn how to recognize quality EVOO: varieties, production methods, tasting techniques and food pairings. An eye-opening and delicious experience.
